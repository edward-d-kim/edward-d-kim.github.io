Teaching Swing Dance in La Crosse in 2023

I+E and requires pre-registration, and a class will be cancelled if there are fewer than 3 enrolled. (During Summer 2023, I co-taught swing with Perrin Iacopino on Thursdays at I+E Dance Studio.)
